Physics (simple, understandable)

  • Drive force pushes the car forward (limited by engine + traction).
  • Traction depends on tire grip × surface friction × normal force (weight + downforce).
  • Drag grows with speed² and increases if a spoiler is installed.
  • Rolling resistance always slows you down a little.
  • Slope (grade) helps downhill and hurts uphill.
How to compete
